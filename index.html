Learning HTML: Understanding Heading 6 (<h6>)

Introduction

Welcome to the lesson on <h6>! This heading is the smallest in size compared to all the heading tags, and it is often used for the least important headings in your webpage content.

Explanation

The <h6> tag defines the sixth level of headings in HTML. It's used for very detailed sections under <h5> headings. Placing text between the <h6> and </h6> tags creates a heading.

Example

Letâ€™s see an example in code:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
    <title>Subheading Level 6 Example</title>
</head>
<body>
    <h6>My Favorite Quotes</h6>
</body>
</html>

When you run this code, you'll see "My Favorite Quotes" appear as a small heading on the page.
